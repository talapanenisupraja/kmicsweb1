Established in the year 1995, Keshav Memorial Institute of Commerce and Sciences is the flagship institution of Keshav Memorial Educational Society. Since its inception, the institution has been striving to impart value based education through various courses like B.Com, B.Sc in Under Graduation and M.Com, M.Sc in Post Graduation with diverse streams. We believe in inclusive growth and sustainable development through education which will instill a sense of responsibility and mould the students to be self reliant and responsible citizens of the Nation. With 1836 student force, 64 teaching staff, 33 non teaching and supporting staff , we have evolved as one of the esteemed institutions in Hyderabad.
